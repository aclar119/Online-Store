About Us

2020| The birth of Generic Clothing co.

In February 2020, five college friends from Ottawa came together with a common passion for sustainability and ethics. They were frustrated by the lack of options for consumers looking to buy products that align with their values. Despite the growing awareness of environmental issues, they found it difficult to find brands that prioritize sustainability and ethical production methods. The market was dominated by fast-fashion brands with questionable practices, and the friends were determined to create a better alternative.
Driven by their shared values and concern for the environment, they decided to create their own ecological and ethical brand. With backgrounds in design, marketing, and environmental science, the friends utilized their diverse skill sets to create a brand that not only looked good but also did good.

2022| The Rise of Generic Clothing co.

We are a brand dedicated to promoting sustainability and creating products that make a positive impact on the world. Our commitment to the environment is evident in every aspect of our business, from sourcing local materials to using eco-production methods to minimize our carbon footprint. Our focus on sustainability has helped us stand out in the market and has earned us a following among socially and environmentally conscious consumers. We have expanded our reach by opening several storefronts in and around Ottawa, and despite our growth, we remain committed to our mission. Our customers trust us for our high-quality and stylish products that are also eco-friendly. We strive to inspire others to make positive changes in their own lives by setting a high standard for sustainability. We believe that by making sustainability a top priority, we can make a lasting impact on the world and pave the way for a more sustainable future.

Our Values

Teamwork

The five college friends from Ottawa embody the essence of teamwork. With a common goal of promoting sustainability and ethics in the consumer market, they combined their diverse backgrounds in design, marketing, and environmental science to create a brand that not only looks good but also does good. Their passion for the environment and shared values fueled their teamwork and allowed them to bring their vision to life. This serves as a testament to the power of teamwork and how individuals can achieve greater success by leveraging their strengths and working together towards a common goal. As the previous text stated, the cornerstone of any successful endeavor is teamwork and when individuals work together, they can accomplish more than what any one person could achieve alone.

Customer-Centric

At our online store, we are committed to providing the best possible shopping experience for our customers. We believe that putting our customers first is the key to success, and that's why we prioritize their needs and wants above all else. From the moment they visit our site, our goal is to make their shopping journey as smooth, convenient, and enjoyable as possible. We understand that customers have unique needs and preferences, and that's why we strive to offer a wide range of products and a user-friendly shopping platform.

Community

At our brand, we understand the importance of community and the power of shared values. We recognize that our customers and supporters share a common passion for sustainability and ethics, and we are grateful for their support and commitment. This passion for a better world drives the mission of our brand and inspires us to continue creating products and initiatives that align with our shared values. We believe that by coming together as a community, we can have a greater impact on the world. Our community not only helps us to spread the message of sustainability and ethics, but it also provides valuable feedback and support. Whether through participating in community initiatives, providing reviews and recommendations, or sharing their experiences with others, our community plays an essential role in shaping the future of our brand. Together, we can make a positive impact on the world and inspire others to join the movement.
