About Us

2020| The birth of Generic Clothing co.

In February 2020, five college friends from Ottawa came together with a common passion for sustainability and ethics. They were frustrated by the lack of options for consumers looking to buy products that align with their values. Despite the growing awareness of environmental issues, they found it difficult to find brands that prioritize sustainability and ethical production methods. The market was dominated by fast-fashion brands with questionable practices, and the friends were determined to create a better alternative.
Driven by their shared values and concern for the environment, they decided to create their own ecological and ethical brand. With backgrounds in design, marketing, and environmental science, the friends utilized their diverse skill sets to create a brand that not only looked good but also did good. They source materials locally and use eco-friendly production methods to minimize their carbon footprint. The brand quickly gained a following among socially and environmentally conscious consumers, and the friends have since expanded their reach, opening several storefronts in and around Ottawa. Despite their success, they remain dedicated to their mission of promoting sustainability and creating products that make a positive impact on the world...

2019| The birth of Generic Clothing co.

Our story began in 1812 when Peter Simons arrived from Scotland and settled on a small farm near Quebec City to raise his family of five. In 1840 his son John moved into the city to open a small shop, where he sold dry goods and various imported wares. To make sure his business received the latest in goods from Europe, he made over 70 Atlantic crossings by sailing ship. In those early days, John Simons was already putting an emphasis on quality merchandise and attentive service, setting the stage for the future success of La Maison Simons.
